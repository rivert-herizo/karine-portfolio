Who am I ?

Hi ! My name is Karine. I am a freelance ghostwriter and proofreader based in Madagascar. But I am also a literature and film enthusiast who would love to help you on your next project. A book, a film, a series — you name it !I love the process of creating a good story, from the worldbuilding to the characters ; all the plot scheming and unpredictable arcs. However, throughout my years on the job market, I found another passion in helping and assisting others to bring their ideas to life. I have seen that making the creating process a teamwork can really elevate its result.

I believe stories and tales to be powerful tools that can deeply affect people's lives. Thus, writing them has to be done properly. Which is why I am here, to help make your vision a reality.
